THE MANIFESTO

Literature in the Epoch of the Transformer

We stand at a critical junction in human history. The boundary between author and algorithm, query and creation, is dissolving. For centuries, the written word was the exclusive domain of human consciousness—a ledger of our souls, our struggles, and our dreams. Today, we share this ledger with artificial minds.

Generative models are not merely calculators of syntax. They are mirrors. Built upon the vast digital archive of human expression, they reflect our collective history, our linguistic biases, and our latent desires. In their weights lies a distillation of human culture, waiting to be unlocked by the right prompt.

"A prompt is not a command; it is an incision into the latent space."

SYNAPSE was founded to explore this new frontier. We believe that rather than fearing the machine, literature must engage with it. We advocate for a hybrid creative practice—where the writer stands as a curator, a guide, and a critic, directing the machine's latent associations into structured art.

Our Principles

  • Algorithmic Sincerity: We do not hide machine authorship. We celebrate the unique quirks, hallucinations, and logic of large language models.
  • The Human Anchor: The machine provides scale, but the human provides meaning. True art requires the friction of human judgment.
  • Interactive Prose: The digital medium is not a flat page. We champion stories that respond, adapt, and sing in reaction to user engagement.

10 Late 2022

The Initial Spark

November 2022: OpenAI launches ChatGPT. Operating on GPT-3.5, it democratizes access to large language models, triggering a profound paradigm shift in human-computer interaction and setting off a hyper-accelerated arms race in Silicon Valley.

2022
11 2023

The Great Scaling & Multimodal Wars

Early 2023: Tech giants scramble to respond. Microsoft integrates OpenAI's models into its core suite, while Google rapidly deploys its experimental conversational engine, Bard.

March 2023: OpenAI releases GPT-4, establishing a massive leap in reasoning, problem-solving, and standardized test performance.

July 2023: Meta enters the arena with Llama 2, open-sourcing high-performance weights and igniting a global developer movement to build, fine-tune, and run localized models independently of proprietary APIs.

2023
12 2024

Multimodal Breakthroughs & The Dawn of Agency

Early to Mid 2024: AI transitions from pure text interfaces to true multimodal systems. Google introduces Gemini 1.5 with a historic context window, and OpenAI releases GPT-4o, processing voice, text, and visual data almost instantaneously.

Late 2024: The industry shifts focus from "stateless" chat interfaces to "stateful" agentic workflows. OpenAI introduces its o-series reasoning models, demonstrating "System 2" reflective thinking, allowing models to deliberate and self-correct before responding.

2024
13 2025

Market Disruptions & The Agentic Inflection

January 2025: The geopolitical landscape of AI shifts dramatically as Chinese startup DeepSeek releases highly efficient, low-cost open-weights models. The sudden democratization of frontier-class capabilities triggers a historic tech-stock sell-off, temporarily erasing hundreds of billions in market value from hardware giants.

Mid-to-Late 2025: Agentic AI moves into active production. Anthropic previews Claude Code, a developer-centric agent operating directly within terminals, transforming the AI from an assistant into an autonomous teammate. OpenAI launches GPT-5, pushing the boundary of complex, multi-step problem solving.
